About TechMeeco Infrastructure Private Limeted

We are focused on delivering custom pre-engineered building (PEB) solutions, offering end-to-end services in design, fabrication, and installation. Our structures ensure faster construction, reduced costs, and customizable designs to meet specific project needs. Committed to innovation, safety, and environmental protection, we deliver reliable, economical steel structures with the highest professionalism..

Genesis of Tech Meeco Infrastructure Private Limited:

We are Meeco Engineering, specializing in steel structural fabrication and manufacturing with a strong presence in the Eastern region. With partnerships like L&T, GE Power India Ltd, and more, we operate four units in the Durgapur area, producing 500 to 600 metric tons of finished materials per month. Our new venture, Tech Meeco Infra Pvt Ltd, focuses on Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB) solutions, offering prefab components to meet specific project needs. Our skilled team ensures high-quality services and timely delivery using the latest industry technologies.
